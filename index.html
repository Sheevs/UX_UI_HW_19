Hello, I’m Sheeva Ayati!

I’m a highly motivated UX/UI designer with a background in accounting. I have a deep appreciation for the creative aspects of design and enjoy collaborating with others to craft user-centered solutions.

I'm passionate about design because it allows me to explore aesthetics, color, and collaborate with teams, all while having the freedom to channel my creativity. 

My strongest skills lie in designing the solution and end product, where I excel in user research, user empathy, and crafting intuitive user interfaces.

When I'm not immersed in UX/UI design, I’m expressing my creativity through painting, watching basketball, yoga, and playing with my furry cats.

Thank you for taking the time to explore my portfolio, and I look forward to the possibility of collaborating!
